$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{a}{b}:\frac{c}{d}&=\frac{\frac{a}{b}}{\frac{c}{d}}\\\\\\
&=\frac{\frac{a}{b}\cdot \frac{d}{c}}{\frac{c}{d}\cdot \frac{d}{c}} \qquad \left(\text{mit } \frac{d}{c} \text{ erweitert} \right)\\\\\\
&=\frac{\frac{a}{b}\cdot \frac{d}{c}}{1}\\\\\\
&=\frac{a}{b}\cdot \frac{d}{c}
\end{aligned}
$$
